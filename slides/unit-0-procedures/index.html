bell to bell

Five steps to starting class

  1. Enter the classroom, picking up materials from the front table.
  2. Take your seat and get out your materials.
  3. When the music stops, begin the Reactant silently and independently for 2 minutes.
  4. When the music starts, you may discuss the questions with your neighbor and ask your teacher questions.
  5. When the music stops again, bring your attention to the front of the class.

Getting the whole class's attention

Your teacher raises a hand and says, “Voices off, eyes up front.” The music stops and the red light comes on.

🔊Let's practice! When your teacher says “GO,” make as much noise as possible. When the attention-getter goes up… silence — all eyes up front.

Unit 0 · Procedureschemlessons.xyz
no such thing as "I don't know"

Answering questions

In this classroom, we DO NOT SAY “I don't know.” Instead, we always do one of two things:

  • Ask a question
  • Give our best attempt at an answer
Unit 0 · Procedureschemlessons.xyz
handle with care

Whiteboard procedures

  • Do not use unless instructed.
  • Keep markers capped when not in use, and be gentle with them.
  • Put markers inside the socks when you're done.
  • Never doodle, draw, write notes, or otherwise waste marker ink.
