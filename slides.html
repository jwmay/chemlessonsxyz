Slide Decks

Lesson slides come in two flavors: classic Google Slides you can copy and edit (find those inside each unit on the curriculum page), and interactive HTML decks hosted right here — no downloads, no logins, they just run in the browser.

HTML decks

These are hand-built web presentations — fast, animated, and projector-friendly. Use the arrow keys, click, or swipe to navigate. More decks are added as units get the HTML treatment.

Presenting a deck

Arrow keys or space bar to advance, F for fullscreen on most browsers, and swipe on touch screens. A progress bar tracks where you are in the lesson.

Prefer Google Slides?

Every lesson also exists as a Google Slides deck you can copy and edit. They're organized by unit on the curriculum page.

Works offline

HTML decks are self-contained single pages — save one with your browser's “Save page” and it presents just fine without internet, projector cart included.
